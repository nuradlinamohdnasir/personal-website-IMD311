What is E-Mail?

Get knowing e-mail: 2021649676@student.uim.edu.my

Email is a shortened form of "electronic mail." It refers to a method of exchanging digital messages over the internet or other computer networks. It allows individuals and organizations to send, receive, and store messages electronically. Just like traditional mail, email allows you to communicate with others by sending written messages, but instead of using paper and envelopes, email uses electronic devices like computers or smartphones to compose, send, and receive messages. It has become a widely used and convenient means of communication, enabling people to stay in touch, share information, and conduct business efficiently.
