Adlina's Website

Challenges Faced

  • There are special tools that help you build and fix websites. It can take some time to learn how to use
    these tools.

  • There are parts of the website that might not look how you want them to. It can be a puzzle to figure out
    what's wrong and how to fix it.

  • Choosing the colors and pictures that look nice on the website can be challenging. It's like picking
    the perfect paint and decorations for your room.

  • Websites may look different on different computers because of the way they are built in your computer.
    It can be tricky to make sure that your website looks nice and works well on all computers.

The Lessons Learned

  • Don't be afraid of making mistakes. It is our opportunities to learn and improve. We can embrace and
    learned from it.

  • Developing a website can test our patience. It's important to be patient and keep trying.

  • Before starting to build a website, it's important to plan and organize ideas. From this, we can be more
    organized and responsible in the future.

  • Staying updated with current web developments is crucial in this era. Many organizations wanted to hire
    someone with variety of knowledge and this could help in finding jobs.
